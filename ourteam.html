Meet Our Team

Founder

Zach Yadegari

TotallyScience.co was founded by Zach Yadegari, a serial entrepreneur, when he was bored during his freshman year of high school. He saw students next to him having multiple tabs open with different game websites and identified the opportunity to make a hub for all of them. He was partially driven by trying to gain popularity in his school. The website spread all around the school and then shortly blew up internationally.

Chief Technology Officer

Ilan Luci

Ilan Luciano is a New York-based programmer specializing in web development. He joined Zach in late 2021 as a freshman in high school to redesign Totally Science.
